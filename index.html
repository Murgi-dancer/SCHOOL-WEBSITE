The history of our SchoolS

in 1989 by Ratna Reddy, CHIREC was founded as a vibrant summer camp for children to develop their skills in arts, sports, and technology. Over the last 30 years, CHIREC International School has grown to become a leader in K-12 education as an institution that instills a passion for learning and innovation. CHIREC International School offers CBSE, IB and Cambridge curricula, and is recognized as one of the best schools in Hyderabad, Telangana and in India.

Safet of kids

At CHIREC`s 5-acre Kondapur campus, our students spend their days in a protected, self-contained environment with separate facilities for Daycare, Primary, Secondary, and Senior Secondary classes, designed especially to suit the requirements of the students. Located near the Botanical Gardens, this campus is in the heart of the IT and Financial district of Hyderabad. The campus provides all the modern facilities and infrastructure for building a sustainable learning environment to create future-ready citizens. Our classrooms are large, spacious, and well-lit. These state-of-the-art, technology-enabled classrooms with ergonomic furniture offer plenty of natural ventilation. Interactive Boards have made it easier to utilize online resources, display 3D models, organize virtual field trips to different parts of the world, and create a rich learning experience for students. CHIREC has a spacious and well-equipped Language Lab, Physics Lab, Chemistry Lab, Biology Lab, Biotechnology Lab, Mathematics Lab, Fashion Studies Lab, and Home Science Lab, in addition to 8 Computer Labs (including Robotics and 3D printing labs). Each laboratory is equipped to handle National and International Curricula, up to Class XII. We have remedial classrooms to help students overcome any learning or writing difficulties. Dedicated remedial counselors are present to provide extra attention to any students who need help. Our open-air Auditorium serves as the stage for the many student-run productions and events hosted by the school. The auditorium is equipped with the latest in sound and lighting technology to provide our students with the best platform to showcase their abilities. Each campus has separate dining areas where a nutritious vegetarian lunch is served every day. The food is prepared by an international organization, Compass, that promises a spread that suits every palate, cooked under stringent health and safety measures. We have two designated First Aid rooms in the CBSE & International section blocks for health-related emergencies. We have nurses in each room to take care of any emergency that may arise.
